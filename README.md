# AI Internship Finder

> Reality-driven AI internship matching system for practical career growth.

---

##  Problem

Students repeatedly apply to internships they are not realistically eligible for due to:
- Inflated job descriptions
- Keyword-based job portals
- Lack of skill gap visibility
- No structured improvement roadmap

This leads to rejection cycles and wasted effort.

---
